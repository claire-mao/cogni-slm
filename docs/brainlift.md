# BrainLift Summary: Cogni AP Tutor

## Thesis
Consistency is the problem, not raw capability. A small model can be valuable if it reliably follows a strict teaching protocol that prompted base models do not follow consistently.

## Spiky POVs
1. Fine-tuning is justified when protocol adherence under pressure is the target.
2. Analogy quality is the learning unit; label-only answers are insufficient.
3. Adversarial robustness is required evidence, not optional polish.

## Research Anchors
- Analogical reasoning and structure mapping.
- Transfer learning and desirable difficulties.
- Self-explanation and metacognitive reflection.
- Instruction tuning and LLM-as-judge evaluation.

## Application to Cogni
The model must preserve a deterministic seven-step tutoring flow, force transfer reasoning, and avoid direct answer leakage. Dataset quality is the primary lever.
